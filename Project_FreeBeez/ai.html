ABOUT GRAPHICS DESIGN

Freelance graphic designers craft visual solutions for clients, spanning logos, branding, web design, and more. They juggle creativity with technical skills, using software like Adobe Creative Suite to design captivating visuals. Freelancers manage their projects, from client communication to deadlines, often balancing multiple gigs. Building a strong portfolio and fostering client relationships are crucial for success. Networking through platforms like Behance or Dribbble and marketing via social media helps attract new clients. Flexibility and adaptability are key in this competitive field, where staying updated on design trends and continuously refining skills is essential for thriving as a freelance graphic designer.

What is creative automation?

Creative automation refers to the use of artificial intelligence technology in the performance of creative production functions. AI optimizes the process in a number of ways: *Quickly analyzes vast amounts of data to generate high volumes of content *Automates repetitive, low-level tasks, e.g. design refinement, size adjustment *Eliminates potential human errors when producing content in the thousands *Simplifies audience personalization and localization with responsive templates
